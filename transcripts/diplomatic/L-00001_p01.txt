April 10-1919

Dearest Darling Blanche,

I received your letter that you wrote
Tuesday yesterday morning about
nine o'clock and it was stamped at 8 30
Tuesday night in Nashville
so it sure did make
a quick trip didn't it.
We past our exam
yesterday and signed
our discharges and also
the pay roll last
night and they say
we will get our discharge
and all out if nothing happens and I
sure hope we get them soon that
morning so I can catch #2 or the
