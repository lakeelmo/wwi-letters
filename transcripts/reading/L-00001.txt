April 10-1919

Dearest Darling Blanche:

I received your letter that you wrote Tuesday yesterday morning about nine o'clock and it was stamped at 8 30 Tuesday night in Nashville so it sure did make a quick trip didn't it. We past our exams yesterday and signed our discharges and also the pay roll last night and they say we will get our discharge and all out if nothing happens and I sure hope we get them soon this morning so I can catch #2 or the

Hope flies out so I can get [illegible] Nashville Sat. eve so we can fly together Sat. night and we can go out home Sunday. Just think only three more days and then we can love each other and just keep on loving each other and not have to be studying about when we have to depart again.

Oh Honey I just want you to always love me and when I am tired or feeling bad I will be perfectly happy if you will just put your arms around me and kiss me.
