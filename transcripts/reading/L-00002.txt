…What family is all about

A brief description follows with names, updates and locations…

Family tree: Frans & Nancy → Lea (Frans, Nicole, Rachel), Auko (Frans), Ingrid, Jorgen (Taylor).

California — Ingrid, formerly in advertising in Los Angeles and Japan with Japan Airlines, now works as a vet technician in Simi Valley (prefers larger animals). Her Jack Russell Terrier is “Little Bear.” Leslie and daughter Taylor jog in Santa Monica. Jorgen balances legal work at Toyota with travel to Japan and hands-on house projects (tools and cacti). Dogs “Big Blue” and “Buffie” (Bouvier des Flandres) welcome little Taylor (Scottie).

On to Texas — “Big Bob” is #1 company representative in the USA and has a talent for the Texas Two Step. Lea does interior design for retirement living facilities in Japan. Rachel shines as a “Star Duster” at junior sports events and senior centers around Austin. Heidi (Dutch Keeshond) and three rabbits round out the household.

Up in Missouri — Big Frans is comfortable in a red enameled Adirondack chair of his own design. Fiancée Lorna claims the longest rambling ivy west of the Mississippi. They celebrated their engagement at the Racquet Club in St. Louis; both practice law (Frans in St. Louis, Lorena in Springfield, Illinois). A kitten from Texas is a new addition.

East Coast — New Haven: Nicole and Eugene are immersed in Yale life; Nicole is with the Yale Endowment Office; after the engagement party they plan Cape Cod, then Mexico City and Acapulco to see Eugene’s family. Cape Cod: Frans Jr. (Aako) juggles work with culinary school in Boston (“Chef de Cuisine”); Laurie rode in the Boston–New York AIDS Bicycle Ride. Boston: another Frans (HoTo) works as a film gaffer; Patricia is an admissions counselor at Emmanuel College (and an excellent salsa dancer); they share life with Siberian Huskies Grindle and Cleo and travel widely.
